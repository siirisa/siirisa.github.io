Hello, stranger!


If you have landed on our page it means you must be a fellow enthusiast for picnics, or maybe just someone looking to escape the chaos of city life for a few hours.

We understand you perfectly, and that is how this website was born. Sometimes life can feel intense and there is no better way to recharge than lay in a field, open a bottle of wine and soak up the sunrays while enjoying good company and food.

More about us? The three of us met in the first year of University in lectures; we study management together and come from Northern Ireland, Finland, and Italy. We want to share our foolproof method of relaxation with whomever needs a timeout from the humdrum of life.
